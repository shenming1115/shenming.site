One day, a dog said to a cat: "Let's compete to see who can spin faster!"
The dog said: "Alright!"

The cat spun and spun, the dog spun and spun, and the cat asked: "Are you dizzy yet?"
The dog said: "Not yet, what about you?"
The cat said: "Me neither, let's keep spinning!"
So they spun and spun again, until finally, the cat stopped and asked: "Why aren't you dizzy yet?"
The dog replied: "Because I'm already 'no dizzy'."
